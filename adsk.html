Autodesk

Cloud-based/desktop digital manufacturing tool

Autodesk is making digital transformation on manufacturing & engineering industry, allowing them to customize production through advances in machine learning & generative design into the manufacturing processes.
At this project, we were in the conceptual stage of building a cloud-based simulation tool for collaborative product development that combines industrial design and machine tool programming into one software solution. Overall, the goal of this project was to create the first version of commercial release used by millions of Autodesk customers.

Design Brief


Problem Statement

After storyboarding and visualizing how might we tackle all possible scenarios on Generative Design tool, I concluded a design brief for my design thinking and validation.

  • It is difficult for customers to guesstimate if the final design solution is machinable in the shop floor. Without manufacturing simulation,the large amount of cost is inevitable.
  • There's a fee occurred when they want to generate a design solution (geometry) from Autodesk Cloud. Without a preview functionality before generating, they are unable to verify until the fee has been made.
  • Most of industrial designers are lacking in confidence to collaborate with simulation analysts, because they have limited knowledge on this filed.
  • At Autodesk, there are numerous product lines across multiple industries, including architecture, engineering, digital entertainment and construction. The first version of Generative Design needs to be aligned with a company-wide Human Interface Guidelines.

hypotheses

With a preview functionality and manufacture(MFG) advisor that can help:

  • Reducing the time and cost on choosing the best final design solution by providing interactive feedback with MFG Advisor
  • Increasing intuition on how a final design solution might look like after adding materials before generating with preview functionality
  • With Generative Design tool, industrial designers can easily collaborate with other mechanical experts.
  • Besides, a set of Generative Design UI guideline should be consistent with the rest of Autodesk products.

Success Metrics

After agreed with stakeholders, they are our success metrics:

  • Reduced manufacturing cost by 50%.
  • 95% of users are able to run previews before generating from Autodesk CLoud.
  • UI can be easily adoptable and reusable in a company-wide human interface guidline.

Solution


Over the discovery and research stage, I explored ideas by sketching as many as possible and turned the best concept into a series of mockups. I then validated the mockups internally across the business as stakeholders could give valuable insights. After some iterations and tweaking based on their feedback, I created a final prototype which I validated on Autodesk customers.

Here is what I concluded for the final design solution:

  • Customers can effortlessly simulate their design solution and select the best result with confidence by Manufacturing Advisor
  • With preview functionality, customers can preview and refine their design solution without a fee before generating any results from Autodek Cloud.
  • A comprehensive tutorial and tooltips should be provided on UI.
  • A design guideline on Generative Design should be provided and aligned with Autodesk product family.
